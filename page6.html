Kinesthesia

Modular Digital Wavetable Synthesiser

'Kinaesthesia' is an interactive sound art installation which uses haptic and tactile feedback via sonic response to guide a participant in their interactions with it. Through this kinaesthetic interaction, the installation attempts to form a simple relationship with the participant; this relationship, over time, is governed by the participant's interaction with the installation, and their reaction to the changing sonic communication from the installation. Kinaesthesia is also "the perception of body position and movement"
