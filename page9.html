Imaginary Seascape No.1

8 Channel Composition

Imaginary Seascape No1 is a 8 channel composition influenced by John Cage's work Imaginary Landscape No1. The work examines notions of spacial orientation and movement, and experiments with the creation of spatial textures facilitated by multi speaker diffusion.
